Quick Study — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 20, 2026

Quick Study does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

When you refresh the card database, the app downloads Magic: The Gathering card data and images directly from Scryfall's public API. These requests go straight from your Mac to Scryfall and are subject to Scryfall's privacy policy; Quick Study sends no personal information with them. All downloaded data is cached locally on your Mac and never leaves it.
